Honey Chipotle Chicken Sandwich

Another low calorie chicken sandwich option. This one is more suited for those with a sweeter pallet.

Ingredients

  • 1 Honey Chipotle Chicken Thigh
  • 1 english muffin
  • 30g light sour cream
  • 5g light mayo
  • 1-2 tspn chives
  • Garlic powder

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pre heat, air fryer to 400 degrees. Lightly moisturize the interior of you english muffin, close it, then lightly spray the exterior with oil and add to the air fryer for `7-8 mins. This will crisp up the exterior, and steam the interior
  2. In a small bowl, combine light sour cream, light mayo, a dash of garlic powder, chives and mix
  3. Place sauce on each half of the english muffin, add on your honey chipotle chicken thigh, top with more chives, and enjoy!
