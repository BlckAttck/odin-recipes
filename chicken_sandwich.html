Chicken Sandwich Recipe

A low calorie high protein dish that is sure to make you full while helping you lose weight.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz chicken breast
  • 1 bag quest ranch chips
  • 1 beaten egg + 1 tblspn buffalo sauce
  • 10g of flour + salt, garlic, paprika
  • light butter
  • honey
  • 2 Brioch Buns
  • 6 pickle slices
  • Bolthouse Farms Ranch

Cooking Instructions

  1. Tenderize chicken breast, trim, and slice in to two pieces
  2. Set up dipping stations and mix all ingredients
  3. Coat both chicken pieces with flour/seasoning mixture then dip in egg , followed by, chip/panko mixture. Place on aire fryer rack
  4. Air fryer at 375 for 10 minutes
  5. Prep buffalo sauce coating by mixing each ingredient together, and microwaving 10-15 seconds to help mix
  6. Remove chicken from air fryer and dip each in Buffalo sauce mixture, then place back on to air fryer rack. Drizzle leftover sauce on top of chicken before placing back in air fryer (if you have more leftover, you can place it on the buns or use as a dip for the sandwiches)
  7. Air fry at 375 for ~4 mins
  8. Add to toasted buns, top with pickles and ranch, and enjoy!!
